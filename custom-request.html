Custom Requests

Collaborative artifact creation

How Custom Requests Work

Custom artifacts begin with a conversation, not a specification. I'm interested in the spark that inspired your request—the feeling, need, or curiosity that wants to take physical form through making.

This is not a traditional commission process. Like all my making, custom artifacts emerge from the joy of creation rather than predetermined outcomes. Some concepts may simmer for months before the right approach reveals itself through the making process.

The most successful custom artifacts happen when we both embrace the poetry of making—remaining open to what wants to emerge rather than forcing what we think should exist.

Setting Expectations

No Hard Deadlines

Artifacts cannot be rushed. Your piece will be completed when it's ready, which may take 3-12 months depending on complexity and my current understanding of your needs.

Collaborative Process

You'll be involved in key decisions throughout creation, but the final form will emerge through our dialogue rather than from predetermined specifications.

Quality Over Speed

Every aspect receives the same attention as my regular artifacts. Compromising quality for timeline is never an option.

Unique Outcome

Your artifact will be truly one-of-a-kind. I don't repeat custom designs, ensuring your piece remains completely unique.

What Happens Next

Response: I'll respond within 2-3 weeks to let you know if your request resonates with my current creative direction.

Dialogue: If we move forward, we'll have several conversations to deepen my understanding of your needs and explore possibilities.

Contemplation: Your request will enter my contemplation process, which may take several months before active creation begins.

Updates: I'll provide periodic updates on the artifact's development, including any insights or challenges that arise.

Completion: When your artifact is ready, you'll receive photos and documentation before shipping.
