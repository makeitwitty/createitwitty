Create it Witty

Unique Custom Artifacts

I create unique, numbered artifacts from the pure joy of making. They don't solve grand problems or save the world—they simply make everyday a little more colorful, or black and white if you prefer it that way.

Like poetry, each artifact is layered and open to your own interpretation. They add small moments of visual interest and quiet contemplation to ordinary life, made in limited quantities of 15 to 40 pieces per collection.
